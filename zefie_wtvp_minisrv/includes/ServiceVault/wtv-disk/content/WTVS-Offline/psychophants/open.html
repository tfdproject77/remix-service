Instructions
Psychophants by: Paul Norman



Anti-matter entities have spontaneously appeared inside a fusion reactor. They are burning through the shielding. If they come in contact with the core, there will be a matter/anti-matter reaction: TOTAL ANNIHILATION.
Shut down the reactor by maneuvering the robot pod (SPIDER) to the four corner control stations. Move toward the front of the console to shut it off. Do not run into the entities or the pod will vaporize. If the the inner wall of the reactor is breached, the core will go critical in 30 seconds. Shutting off a station will delay meltdown for 10 seconds. Robot pods will be replaced as needed.

Good luck.
